Pro Tips for PTE

Quick start
✏️ Spelling

Spelling matters – practice writing each word 3 times. Common traps: accommodation, environment, committee.

🔗 Collocations

Collocation matters more than individual words – always learn the word with its common partner (e.g. reach a conclusion, not make a conclusion).

📊 Describe Image

For Describe Image, the words from Group 2 are your "cheat sheet" – use them in every response (fluctuate, peak, soar, plummet).

✍️ Essay & Fill in the Blanks

For Essay, use 2–3 Group 1 words and 2–3 connectors from Group 3 per paragraph. For Fill in the Blanks, Group 3 collocations (especially prepositions) are tested most.
